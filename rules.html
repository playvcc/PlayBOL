Sector 7 Rules

All players and teams must follow these rules to compete fairly.

General Rules

  • No cheating, modding, or exploiting game mechanics.
  • No harassment, racism, or toxic behavior.
  • All players must use their registered username.
  • Match results must be reported accurately.
  • False disputes or reports may result in penalties.

Match Rules

  • Both players must confirm match results.
  • Disconnections count as a loss unless agreed otherwise.
  • Proof is required for disputes (screenshots or video).
  • Matches must follow the selected ladder’s ruleset.

Ladder Rules

  • Teams must have at least 4 members for 4v4 ladders.
  • Sniper ladder is solo only.
  • Seasonal ladders reset every season.
  • Boosting XP or ELO is strictly prohibited.

Penalties

  • Match reversals
  • XP/ELO deductions
  • Temporary suspensions
  • Permanent bans for repeated offenses

Rule Info

Rules apply to all ladders and match types.

Admins may update rules at any time.

Tips

Record your matches for proof.

Communicate clearly with opponents.
