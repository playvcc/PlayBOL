Casual Division

The Casual Division is for players who want fun, not competition.

  • Open lobbies
  • Zombies / custom games
  • Fun community nights
  • No pressure matchmaking
  • Learning environment for new players
  • LFG (Looking For Group)

Casual Rules

  • Respect all members
  • No cheating or exploiting
  • No toxicity or harassment
  • Follow Discord/community rules

Competitive Division

Structured ranked system for serious players.

  • 4v4 / 5v5 matches
  • BO1 regular season
  • BO3 playoffs
  • Objective-based competitive modes only

Team Structure

Each team:

  • 1 Captain – manages roster and matches
  • 1–2 Officers – recruitment & coordination
  • 4–10 Players – active participation

Team Leagues

  • Bronze League
  • Silver League
  • Gold League
  • Platinum League
  • Elite Division

Season Flow

  • Weeks 1–6: Ranked matches
  • Week 7: Qualification phase
  • Week 8: Playoffs & Finals

Legacy rewards remain permanent even when ranks reset.

Legacy Rewards

  • Special roles
  • Hall of Fame entry
  • Profile badges
  • Priority team placement
  • Exclusive events
