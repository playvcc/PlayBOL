THE COMPETITIVE PLAYLIST

Control rotating hardpoints. 250 points wins. Coordination, rotations, and clutch plays decide the match.

Objective

One life. No respawns. Plant or defuse. Six rounds wins the half. The most mental game mode ever made.

Elimination

Capture the Flag

Grab the enemy flag and return it to yours. Three captures wins. Speed, escorting, and flag defense are everything.
