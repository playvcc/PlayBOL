1. Eligibility

1.1 General Requirements

Any player may sign up for BO2 League. Registration is free and open to all. Players must complete sign-up before the close of the registration period each season.

Players must have an active Xbox or Plutonium (PC) account capable of verification by league staff. Shared or borrowed accounts are not permitted.

1.2 Combine Requirement

To be draft-eligible, players must complete a minimum of three (3) combine matches during the combine period. Combines are the primary method by which GMs scout and evaluate players.

⚠ Players who do not meet the combine minimum will not be eligible for the franchise draft, but may still join as Free Agents (FA) or Subs.

1.3 Platform Eligibility

BO2 League supports two platforms:

  • Xbox (original hardware or backwards-compatible)
  • PC via Plutonium BO2 client (free, anti-cheat enabled)

2. League Structure

2.1 Tiers

  • Recruit — Entry level
  • Prospect — Developing
  • Contender — Intermediate
  • Challenger — Advanced
  • Elite — Expert
  • Prestige — Pinnacle

2.2 Franchises

Each franchise is led by a General Manager (GM) and up to two Assistant GMs. Franchises field teams across multiple tiers.

2.3 Free Agents

Unsigned players may participate as Free Agents. FAs can substitute for signed players who are unavailable for a given match week.

3. Player Conduct

3.1 Sportsmanship

All players must conduct themselves respectfully toward opponents, teammates, staff, and the community at all times.

3.2 Prohibited Behavior

  • Cheating, hacking, or exploiting glitches
  • Harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory language
  • Match manipulation or intentional losing
  • Sharing account credentials
  • Publicly disparaging staff decisions before the appeals process

🚫 1st offense: warning. 2nd offense: match suspension. 3rd offense: season ban. Severe violations result in immediate permanent removal.

4. Match Rules

4.1 Format

Regular season: Best of 1. Playoffs: Best of 3. Championship Final: Best of 5.

4.2 Game Modes

  • Hardpoint — 250 points to win
  • Search & Destroy — 6 rounds wins the half
  • Capture the Flag — 3 captures wins

4.3 Approved Maps

  • Hardpoint: Raid, Standoff, Turbine, Express, Slums, Overflow
  • S&D: Raid, Standoff, Carrier, Hijacked, Yemen, Plaza
  • CTF: Raid, Slums, Hijacked, Express, Turbine

4.4 Scheduling

Teams must be ready within 15 minutes of match start. Failure to field 5 players results in a forfeit.

5. Scoring

Win = 3 points. Forfeit win = 3 points. Forfeit loss = 0. Tiebreakers: (1) head-to-head record, (2) map differential, (3) kill differential.

6. Penalties

Penalties are issued by league staff and the Conduct Committee. Point deductions, match suspensions, or season bans may apply depending on severity.

7. Playoffs

Top teams per tier qualify based on regular season standing. Single-elimination for QF and SF; championship is a standalone BO5. No roster changes once the playoff period begins.

8. Appeals

Any player or team may appeal a ruling within 48 hours by submitting an appeal ticket in the BO2 League Discord. Arbiter Committee decisions are final.

📋 Open a ticket in #roles-and-tickets with subject: "APPEAL — [Gamertag] — [Brief Description]"
