1. Eligibility

1.1 General Requirements

Any player may sign up for BO2 League. Registration is free and open to all. Players must complete sign-up before the close of the registration period each season.

Players must have an active Xbox or Plutonium (PC) account capable of being verified by league staff. Shared or borrowed accounts are not permitted.

1.2 Combine Requirement

To be draft-eligible, players must complete a minimum of three (3) combine matches during the combine period. Combines are the primary method by which General Managers scout and evaluate players.

⚠ Players who do not meet the combine minimum will not be eligible for the franchise draft, but may still join as Free Agents (FA) or Subs during the season.

1.3 Platform Eligibility

BO2 League supports two platforms:

  • Xbox (original hardware or backwards-compatible console)
  • PC via Plutonium BO2 client (free, anti-cheat enabled)

Players must declare their platform at sign-up. Platform changes between seasons are permitted; mid-season platform changes require staff approval.

2. League Structure

2.1 Tiers

The league is divided into six competitive tiers, listed from lowest to highest:

  • Recruit — Entry level. New competitive players.
  • Prospect — Developing players with solid fundamentals.
  • Contender — Intermediate players with consistent performance.
  • Challenger — Advanced players with strong mechanics.
  • Elite — Expert players. Top of most seasons.
  • Prestige — The pinnacle of BO2 League competition.

2.2 Franchises

Each franchise is led by a General Manager (GM) and up to two Assistant General Managers (AGMs). Franchises field teams in multiple tiers, building cohesive organizations across skill levels.

2.3 Free Agents

Players who are not signed to a franchise may participate as Free Agents (FA). FAs can be called up to substitute for signed players who are unavailable for a given match week.

3. Player Conduct

3.1 Sportsmanship

All players are expected to conduct themselves with respect toward opponents, teammates, staff, and the broader community at all times — in matches, in Discord, and in any BO2 League-affiliated space.

3.2 Prohibited Behavior

  • Cheating, hacking, or exploiting glitches for competitive advantage
  • Harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory language of any kind
  • Match manipulation or intentional losing
  • Sharing account credentials or playing on another player's account
  • Publicly disparaging league staff decisions before the appeals process

🚫 First offense: warning. Second offense: match suspension. Third offense: season ban. Severe violations (cheating, hate speech) result in immediate permanent removal.

4. Match Rules

4.1 Format

Regular season matches are Best of 1 (BO1). Playoff matches are Best of 3 (BO3). Championship finals are Best of 5 (BO5).

4.2 Game Modes (Competitive Rotation)

  • Hardpoint — 250 points to win
  • Search & Destroy — 6 rounds wins the half
  • Capture the Flag — 3 captures wins

4.3 Approved Maps

  • Hardpoint: Raid, Standoff, Turbine, Express, Slums, Overflow
  • Search & Destroy: Raid, Standoff, Carrier, Hijacked, Yemen, Plaza
  • CTF: Raid, Slums, Hijacked, Express, Turbine

4.4 Scheduling

Matches are scheduled by staff and communicated via Discord. Teams must be ready to play within 15 minutes of match start time. Failure to field a full roster (5 players) within 15 minutes results in a forfeit.

5. Scoring

Regular season standings are determined by match wins. Each match win earns 3 points; a forfeit win earns 3 points; a forfeit loss earns 0 points. Tiebreakers are resolved by: (1) head-to-head record, (2) map differential, (3) total kills differential.

6. Penalties

Penalties are issued by league staff and the Conduct Committee. Players and teams may receive point deductions, match suspensions, or full season bans depending on the severity of violations. All penalties are logged and made available to relevant parties.

7. Playoffs

The top teams in each tier qualify for the playoffs based on regular season standing. Playoff bracket format is single-elimination for quarterfinals and semifinals, with the championship being a standalone best-of-five event.

Playoff seeding is locked at the end of the regular season. No trades, signings, or roster changes are permitted once the playoff period begins.

8. Appeals

Any player or team may appeal a ruling within 48 hours of a decision by submitting an appeal ticket in the BO2 League Discord. Appeals are reviewed by the Arbiter Committee, which operates independently of match staff. Arbiter decisions are final.

📋 To submit an appeal, open a ticket in #roles-and-tickets in the Discord server with the subject "APPEAL — [Your Gamertag] — [Brief Description]".
