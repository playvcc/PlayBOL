General Rules

  1. Respect all players and staff.
  2. No toxicity, racism, or harassment.
  3. No cheating or exploiting.
  4. Stay active if in competitive teams.
  5. Follow Discord and communication rules.

Enforcement System

  • 1st Violation: Warning
  • 2nd Violation: Suspension
  • 3rd Violation: Removal from team
  • Severe Violations: Immediate removal from Sector 7

Philosophy

  • Gameplay over politics
  • Fair competition
  • Active participation
  • Structured progression

Avoids favoritism, inactive teams, messy leadership, and lack of structure.
