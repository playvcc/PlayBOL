Community Overview

Sector 7 is a structured BO2 clan network with two core divisions:

  • Casual Division: Open lobbies, zombies/custom games, fun nights, LFG, no pressure.
  • Competitive Division: Ranked teams, 4v4/5v5, BO1 regular season, BO3 playoffs.

Community Ranks

  • Recruit0–499 XP
  • Member500–1,499 XP
  • Competitor1,500–4,999 XP
  • Elite5,000–9,999 XP
  • Officer10,000–24,999 XP
  • Captain25,000–49,999 XP

Progression is based on activity, performance, and team contribution—no favoritism.

Season System

Each season lasts 6–8 weeks:

  • Weeks 1–6: Ranked ladder matches
  • Week 7: Qualification phase
  • Week 8: Playoffs & Finals

Ranks reset each season, legacy rewards remain permanent.
